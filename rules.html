Lottery System

Format

To determine the winner, fourteen ping pong balls numbered 1–14 are placed in a standard lottery machine and four balls are randomly selected from the lot. Just as in most traditional lotteries, the order in which the numbers are drawn is not important. That is, 1–2–3–4 is considered to be the same as 4–3–2–1. There are a total of 1,001 combinations (or 14! / (10! x 4!)). Of these combinations, one outcome is disregarded and the remaining outcomes are distributed among the 4 non-playoff teams.

Seeding

Seeded 1-4 in reverse order of last year’s standings.

Example : 9th Place Finisher is Seed 1, 12th Place Finisher is Seed 4

Determination

Lottery is only to award 1st overall pick. The Following picks (2-4) will be distributed passed off seeding. Highest remaining seed would get next pick.

Example : Seed 2 wins Lottery. Pick 2 would go to Seed 1, Pick 3 to Seed 3, Pick 4 to Seed 4.

Seed 2: 30% (501-801 Combinations)

Seed 3: 20% (802-902 Combinations)

Seed 4: 10% (903-1001 Combinations)
